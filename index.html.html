Radial Stress in Plate with a Hole — ML vs. Analytical

Intent/Intro: CMU coursework: Predict radial stress around a circular hole under uniaxial tension using linear regression to infer σ₀ and R from measured data.

Method: Collected stress data at r, fit closed‑form model via least squares, validated on held‑out points.

Results: Predicted field matched theory; generalized to new loads.

Discussion/Contribution: Built dataset & regression, derived evaluation scripts, wrote analysis; implications for quick design checks.

Buckling of Heterogeneous Columns — SVM Classifier

Contribution: Model design, ablations, and interpretability plots.
