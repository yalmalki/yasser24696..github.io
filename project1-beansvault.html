BEANSVAULT | Smart Coffee Bean Preservation System

Introduction

Many coffee enthusiasts buy high-quality beans but store them using bags or jars that allow air, light, and moisture to slowly degrade flavor. BEANSVAULT is a smart, airtight container and service that helps users preserve bean quality by monitoring freshness and providing simple, guided storage feedback. The goal of this project was to design and evaluate a system that extends coffee freshness and reduces waste through better storage and clearer user guidance.

Methods / Design

  • Interviewed coffee drinkers about storage habits and pain points.
  • Defined requirements for seal quality, durability, usability, and capacity.
  • Sketched multiple hardware concepts and selected an airtight, stackable form.
  • Outlined IoT architecture using low-power humidity and temperature sensors.
  • Designed simple app wireframes showing roast date, freshness timeline, and reminders.
  • Created a basic subscription model for scheduled bean deliveries based on usage.

Results / Evaluation

A low-fidelity prototype was created, combining an airtight container with mock sensors and a basic freshness dashboard. A short pilot with a few users suggested improved aroma retention and clearer guidance about when beans were past their ideal window. The results confirmed that combining improved storage with simple freshness feedback can meaningfully improve the home brewing experience.

Discussion / My Contribution

I led the end-to-end design effort, including problem definition, user research, hardware concept design, sensing architecture, and app wireframes. I also drafted the subscription model and conducted a small pilot to evaluate the solution. Based on user feedback, I recommended next steps such as prototyping real electronics, refining the industrial design, and adding predictive models to estimate optimal brewing windows.

Overall, this project demonstrates the integration of engineering principles, user experience design, and business thinking to create a product with real social and practical value.
